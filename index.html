Preventative Care

What is a Pelvic Floor MOT?

Ever wonder if everything is functioning as it should be? Want to learn more about how your pelvis works?

This is a one-off specialist session designed for women who don't currently experience symptoms but want to take a proactive approach to their pelvic health.

Many pelvic health issues develop over a long period of time, and can be prevented. A pelvic floor MOT allows you to understand how your pelvic floor is functioning now, and what you can do to maintain long-term health, as well as prevent future problems.

What This Session Includes

  • Internal vaginal examination to assess your pelvic floor
  • Identifying any early signs of weakness, tension or dysfunction
  • Core strength assessment
  • Personalised advice and treatment plans tailored to your lifestyle

Most women only need a single session, with no ongoing treatment required.

Postnatal Care

Postnatal Assessment

Your postnatal body deserves time, care, and expert support. A postnatal assessment is here to help you understand your recovery, address any concerns, and guide you safely back to the activities you love.

We'll start with a conversation about your pregnancy, birth, and how you've been feeling since. You might want to discuss things like pelvic floor symptoms (leaking, heaviness), abdominal separation, pain, returning to exercise, or simply checking that everything is recovering well.

Following your assessment, you'll receive a personalised plan tailored to your body, your symptoms, and your goals. This may include targeted exercises, practical advice, and guidance on returning to exercise, lifting, and everyday activities with confidence.

This is a space to be listened to, supported, and guided — so you can feel strong, informed, and reassured in your recovery.

The physical assessment may include

  • Posture, breathing, and how your body moves
  • Checking your abdominal muscles, including for any separation
  • Pelvic floor assessment for strength, coordination, and control
  • Assessment of any scars (perineal or C-section), if relevant

Everything is explained clearly, and you are always in control — nothing is carried out without your consent.
